About Us

Wild Rose Pushkar offers a variety of cuisines ranging from traditional Rajasthani to European and continental and Inter-continental Food range from all sides of the world. The focus is on the use of fresh homegrown ingredients which ensures that the dishes are fresh and healthy. In addition to 24 rooms, wild rose Pushkar also has 12 luxury camps and all-day restaurant with views of the verdant garden and the Aravali's We organise camel riding, yoga, hill hiking, campfire, sand dams, and short trips to Aravali hills nearby. We provide private barbeque service from farm to table we have an all-inclusive offer also
